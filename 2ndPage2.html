Reducing The Impact On Our Environment

What are some ways to reduce the environmental impact of computer use?

Save Electricity:
You must've heard this so many times before, but it's true! Turn off all of your devices when not in use, it conserves energy and electricity.
Update The Software, Don't Upgrade The Hardware:
If you ask me, there isn't much of a difference between a Windows 10 and a Windows 11. What really matters are the software functions, so instead of wasting money to buy another computer, just upgrade!
