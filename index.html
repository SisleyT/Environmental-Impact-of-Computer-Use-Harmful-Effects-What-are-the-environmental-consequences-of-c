Harmful Effects

What are the environmental consequences of computer device production?

Resource Extraction:
Mining for metals such as lithium, cobalt, and rare earth elements (the things that are needed to produce computer parts.) can damage ecosystems and lead to habitat destruction!
Over consumption:
Tons of upgrades and changes made in a short period of time together leads to more devices being produced, which leads to more waste in landfills!
Energy:
The manufacturing process for making computers itself uses tons of electricity and mostly non-renewable resources (minerals, plastic, oil, etc.)
