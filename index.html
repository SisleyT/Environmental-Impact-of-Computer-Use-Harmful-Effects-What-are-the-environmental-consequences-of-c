Environmental Impact

  1. Harmful Effects

  2. What are the environmental consequences of computer device production?
  3. Resource Extraction: Mining for metals such as lithium, cobalt, and rare earth elements (the things that are needed to produce computer parts.) can damage ecosystems and lead to habitat destruction!
  4. Over consumption: Tons of upgrades and changes made in a short period of time together leads to more devices being produced, which leads to more waste in landfills!
  5. Energy: The manufacturing process for making computers itself uses tons of electricity and mostly non-renewable resources (minerals, plastic, oil, etc.)
